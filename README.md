Graduated in Digital Design from Anhembi Morumbi University, I focus on front-end development with a strong interest in building immersive, visually engaging digital experiences.

I am currently focused on continuous learning and hands-on practice, exploring modern front-end technologies and workflows. Through personal and experimental projects, I work with React and frameworks such as Vite and Next.js, integrating animations, interactions, and performance-focused solutions while constantly challenging myself to evolve as a developer.

#### In Progress

Building an immersive, interactive landing page experience to showcase a mechanical keyboard, exploring 3D interaction, motion, and user-driven customization.
